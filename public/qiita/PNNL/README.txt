Dynamics of the gut microbiome in Inflammatory Bowel Disease - ID 1629
Jansson_Lamendella_Crohns
Abstract

We elucidated the dynamics of the gut microbiome from in an IBD cohort consisting of 118 individuals (49 CD, 60 UC, 9 healthy controls; HC) at three-month intervals, ranging from 1 to 10 samples per individual for a total of 555 samples.
